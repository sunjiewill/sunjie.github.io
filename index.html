Short BIO

Dr. Jie Sun received the Ph.D. degree from Beihang University, and studied under Beihang Prof. Feng Liu and Tsinghua Prof. Yong Li. He currently belongs to Prof. Fu Xiao's team, and is an Assistant Professor with the School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research interests include future network, artificial intelligence, network function virtualization, and software-defined networking, etc.

Projects

  • Research on Intelligent Placement Technologies of Virtual Service Function Chains in Space-Terrestrial Integrated Networks (NSFC Grant Nos. 62302231), Host, 2024.01-2026.12
    As an important infrastructure for 6G “Ubiquitous Access” and “Intelligence of Everything”, the Space-Terrestrial Integrated Network (STIN) is of great significance to China's economic development and national defense security. Although existing work has made active exploration, there is still a lack of in-depth research on the virtualization, intelligence, and scale methods of constructing STIN services. Therefore, this project plans to carry out research on intelligent placement technologies for virtual Service Function Chains (SFCs) in STIN. Specifically, by establishing an accurate, generalized, and efficient STIN learning framework, we aim to provide continuous, stable, reliable, robust, accurate, and real-time STIN services. The expected research outcomes of this project will achieve a more intelligent STIN, and better serve all industries of China's economy.

  • Integration and Comprehensive Verification of Key Technologies of Space Integrated Information Network Based on Near Space Platform (Grant Nos. NY223039), Host, 2023.07-2026.06
    This project is to design a space integrated regional networking communication and cooperative monitoring architecture with adjacent space platforms and satellites as backbone nodes. Therefore, it has the following responsibilities, e.g., designing a software defined integrated network architecture of sensing, transmission, computing and storage based on the near space platform; designing a heterogeneous interconnection architecture of the space-earth network based on SDN abstract unified model; designing DTN protocol based on TCP socket and Libcap; designing the software bus technology of the multi-type load interface of the space station; implementing the virtual network card drivers of CAN card, RS232 card, RS422 card, etc. based on Linux C and kernel programming; and completing the unified abstract description of the multi-type heterogeneous on-board resources of the space station.
