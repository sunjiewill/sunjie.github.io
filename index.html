Short BIO

Dr. Jie Sun received the BS degree in computer and information engineering college from Huaiyin Institute of Technology, Huaian, China, in 2012, the MS degree in the school of computer science from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Nanjing, China, in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2022, respectively. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research interests include software-defined networking, network function virtualization, data center network, and the space-ground integrated network, etc.

We are also looking talented BS/MS/Ph. D. students, and visiting scholars who are interested in working in our lab.

Projects

  • Integration and comprehensive verification of key technologies of space space integrated information network based on near space platform (NSFC Grant Nos. 91738301), Participation, 2018.01-2021.12
    Description: A space space integrated regional networking communication and cooperative monitoring architecture with adjacent space platforms and satellites as backbone nodes.

    Description: On orbit verification method of key technologies of space information network based on space station engineering and aerospace technology test task.
    Responsibilities: The software bus technology of the multi type load interface of the space station is designed, and the virtual network card drivers of CAN card, RS232 card, RS422 card, etc. are implemented based on Linux C and kernel programming, and the unified abstract description of the multi type heterogeneous on-board resources of the space station is completed.
